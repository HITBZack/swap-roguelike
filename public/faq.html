FAQ

How do I equip items?
Right-click an item in your Inventory, or use the Equip button in the item details panel.

Do I lose items on death?
No. Equipped items persist; runs consume no inventory.

What does Blessing do?
Blessed items have doubled effective stacks for the run.

Is combat deterministic?
Yes, seeded RNG ensures reproducible results per seed.
